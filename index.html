News and highlights:
  • [June 2024] We have welcomed our third cohort of Irvine Summer Institute in Biostatistics and Undergraduate Data Science (ISI-BUDS) participants. The cohort consists of 25 undergraduate students from all over the country. Group members Catalina Medina and Isaac Goldstein teach and mentor ISI-BUDS participants this year.
  • [June 2024] This summer group members Thanasi Bakis, Christian Bernal Zelaya, and Jessalyn Sebastian are participating in an internship program at the CDC Center for Forecasting and Outbreak Analytics. Excited for them to be gaining new insights into public health research and policy!
  • [May 2024] Isaac Goldstein defended his dissertation. He is starting his postdoctoral appointment at Stanford Department of Statistics in Summer 2024. Even before moving to Stanford Isaac managed to have won a competitively awarded Postdoctoral Fellowship from Stanford Center for Computational, Evolutionary and Human Genomics. Double congrats to Isaac!
  • [June 2024] Thanasi Bakis has become a PhD candidate by successfully passing his Advancement to Candidacy exam! Congrats!
