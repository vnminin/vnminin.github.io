  • [03/28/2019] Michael Karcher, Marc Suchard, Gytis Dudas and I have a sequel to our preferential sampling model/paper: Estimating effective population size changes from preferentially sampled genetic sequences. Now implemented in BEAST and with ability to include time-varying covariates.
